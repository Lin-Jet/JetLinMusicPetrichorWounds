Petrichor Wounds By Malnutrition

This album was origially going to be a simple 10 minute dark ambient song inspired by The Stalker by Andrei Tarovsky. I was also interested in increasing my music theory knowledge, especially how to create chord progressions so that my song writing skills can improve. Here I was using a lot of secondary dominants to lead into the tonic chord as well as tritone substitutions. Then I made a bunch of note choices based on if they sound cool or not so at the end of the day I have no idea what is going on.

Malnutrition is by dark electronic music project, I have 2 albums and an ep under this project name and they can be found on my Bandcamp
